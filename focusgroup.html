  • Focus Group Transcription

    Here at King Audio Transcription & Typing Services we are very experienced in the field of focus group transcription, having transcribed many, many hours of focus group recordings since we were established in 2003.

    For your ease we calculate prices for focus group transcriptions on a ‘per audio minute’ basis, meaning that you will receive an accurate quote before the start of the project. When you contact us for a quote we will ask for details of your requirements, for example, whether participants in the focus group need to be identified in the transcript. These requirements will allow us to calculate an appropriate price 'per audio minute' based on the complexity of your project.

    All information passed to King Audio Transcription & Typing Services is treated with the utmost confidentiality and a Confidentiality Agreement can be supplied upon request.
